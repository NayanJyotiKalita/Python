# Operations on list

### Given two arrays, some operations are performed on these arrays, and the following three arrays are obtained after the operations as output. What are those operations that are performed on the arrays respectively?

```
arr1 = [1,2,3]
arr2 = [6,5,4]

I.   arr1   = [1,2,3,6,5,4]
II.  arr1   = [1,2,3,[6,5,4]]
III. arr2  = [4,5,6]
```
---

## Choose the correct answer from below:

  - append, extend, sort

  - extend, append, sort

  - append, sort, extend

  - None of the above

---

# Answer
*extend, append, sort*
